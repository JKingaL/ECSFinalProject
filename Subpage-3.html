Banned elements

there are elements that are now banned in figure skating competitions, most of them, because they are too dangerous to perform.
These elements are the following:

  1. Backflip

  2. The deadlift/ headbanger

  3. The detroier

  4. lying down on ice

  5. Any part of the costume detaching

  6. Using props
